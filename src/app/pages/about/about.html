About Purok-Health

A service-first initiative to improve healthcare accessibility in Pampanga.

Our Mission

Purok-Health aims to bridge the gap between residents and healthcare facilities. By providing real-time information about medicine availability and lab test services, we empower people to make informed decisions about where to seek care.

Our platform covers various facilities across Pampanga, from large district hospitals to local barangay health centers.

How it Works

  • 01. Search for specific medicines or services like "X-Ray" or "Amoxicillin".
  • 02. Filter by facility type or category to narrow down your options.
  • 03. Check real-time availability status before heading out.

Community Driven

This initiative is a community-driven project dedicated to leveraging technology for social good. We are committed to making healthcare information accessible to everyone in our local communities.
